Which licence is best for you?

Mainland License

A mainland license allows businesses to operate anywhere in the UAE's mainland. It may require a local partner for certain activities. Ideal for those wanting direct access to the local market.

Free Zone License

A free zone license provides 100% foreign ownership within a specific free zone. While offering various benefits, businesses may be restricted to operating within the free zone itself.

Offshore Licence

An offshore license is suitable for businesses focused on international activities. It provides 100% foreign ownership but prohibits local business operations within the UAE.
